Apple Pie

A flaky, tasty delight

This American classic is the pinnacle of what this great country has to offer. Slimy apples encased in a buttery, brownd shell, topped with homemade vanilla icing, is the perfect compliment to a Fourth of July picnic.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Cinammon (optional)
  • Vodka (not optional)

Our Apple Pie Recipe

  1. Mix apples, sugar, lemon juice, and sugar in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mix flour, butter, and cinammon into a dough. Create one circle and a bunch of stripes.
  3. Drape the circle over the bottom of a prepped pie dish.
  4. Pour in apple mixture.
  5. Layer dough stripes like so.
  6. Leave pie out in the noonday sun for 4 hours and pray.
  7. Eat with a spoonful of homemade vanilla ice cream and a heft mug of vodka.
